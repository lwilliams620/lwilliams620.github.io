Are Black Californians More Likely to be Arrested?

A Comparison of Arrests in California by Ethnicity in 2019

Author: Lukas Williams

Disclaimers:
These are visualizations of arrests, not convictions. Not every arrest will necessarily lead to a conviction, or even a conviction in the same category.
Classification and labeling of ethnicities is subjective.
Population percentages are estimates from 2019.

Black Californians are arrested at a higher rate by population than any other ethnicity.

They make up 6.5% of California's population, but are 16% of arrests.

Sources: Arrest Data Population Estimates

When looking at the type of arrests, Black Californians are arrested for more felonies than other ethnicities.

36.0% of their arrests are for felonies, when the average for all other ethnicities is 26.4%.

Source: Arrest Data

Black Californians are more likely to be arrested for violent felonies, defined as homicide, rape, robbery, assault, and kidnapping.

41% of their arrests are in this category, compared to 36% for all other ethnicities.

Source: Arrest Data
